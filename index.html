Out and About

Car Seat and Stroller

This might be one of the most expensive items you'll purchase before your newborn arrives. But it's also the item that will frustrate you the most if you don't make the right purchase.

As one new mother put it to us, "Don’t fuck around with the stroller. Buy a goodie. I tried to go cheap and ended up returning it. Something that's easy to clean, and rolls like butter with 1 hand. I know this sounds ridiculous but when you’re holding your ice coffee, answering emails and wheeling the little pork chop along, it’s essential."
